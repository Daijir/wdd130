White Rafting is the best Company for Rafting And Treks in Pennsylvania!

Today, White Water Rafting stands as a testament to the spirit of adventure. Our base camp, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, welcomes thousands of guests each season. With over 25 years of experience, we continue to offer unmatched river rafting experiences, exceptional service, and a deep commitment to safety and environmental stewardship.

The River Explorer

Ideal for families and first-time rafters, The River Explorer is a gentle introduction to the world of white-water rafting. As you meander down the serene sections of the River Valley, your guide will share fascinating insights about the local ecosystem, wildlife, and history. This tranquil excursion offers plenty of opportunities to swim, relax, and enjoy the scenic beauty of the surrounding landscapes. It’s more than just rafting; it’s a peaceful adventure that connects you with nature.

The Adventure Seeker

Designed for those with a thirst for adventure, The Adventure Seeker takes you on a thrilling journey through the heart of the river’s dynamic rapids. Prepare for a day filled with exhilarating splashes and heart-stopping drops, all under the expert guidance of our seasoned rafting professionals. This trip includes a riverside lunch, giving you a moment to relax and bond with your fellow rafters amidst the natural beauty. Perfect for adventurous souls and those looking to step up their rafting game, The Adventure Seeker promises an unforgettable day of thrills and laughter.

The Ultimate Challenge

For the most daring adventurers, The Ultimate Challenge is a two-day epic expedition designed to test your limits. Tackling the river’s most ferocious rapids, this adventure is not for the faint-hearted. Day one starts with a comprehensive safety and skills briefing before you plunge into the white-water fury. As the sun sets, you’ll camp along the riverbank, sharing stories of the day’s conquests under the stars. Day two ups the ante with even more challenging rapids. This option includes all meals and camping equipment, ensuring you’re well-fueled and rested for the adventure that awaits. The Ultimate Challenge is a testament to the human spirit’s resilience and the raw power of nature.
